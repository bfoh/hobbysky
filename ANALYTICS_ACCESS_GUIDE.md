## üìà Key Metrics Explained

### ADR (Average Daily Rate)
**Formula**: Total Revenue √∑ Total Nights Sold  
**What it means**: Average price you charge per room per night  
**Good to know**: Higher ADR = better pricing power

### RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)
**Formula**: Total Revenue √∑ Total Available Rooms  
**What it means**: How well you fill rooms at current prices  
**Good to know**: Industry standard performance metric

### Occupancy Rate
**Formula**: (Occupied Rooms √∑ Total Rooms) √ó 100  
**What it means**: Percentage of rooms filled  
**Good to know**: 70-80% is typically healthy

### Repeat Guest Rate
**Formula**: (Returning Guests √∑ Total Guests) √ó 100  
**What it means**: Customer loyalty indicator  
**Good to know**: 30%+ is excellent

### Guest Lifetime Value
**Formula**: Total Revenue from Guest  
**What it means**: How valuable each guest is  
**Good to know**: Focus on high-value guests
